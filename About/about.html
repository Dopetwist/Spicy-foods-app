About Us

Twist's Spicy Foods is a registered Company in Australia established to render services in food, beverages and catering. Over the years, we pursued the vision to be the trademark in hospitality business when you talk in terms of various delicacies.

With carefully selected Oriental and Mediterranean dishes, experienced chefs, and professional staffs, we guarantee that your visit to Twist's Spicy Foods will be worth it.

We are built on total Commitment to Customer Satisfaction, Excellent Service, Loyalty and Trust.
Our Mission is to be the most favorite destination for every Customer who prefers Healthy, Appetizing, Nourishing and Refreshing Meals at all times.

One of the remarkable experiences we have is the aroma of knowledge that seems from knowing what exactly satisfies an appetite. For us, the secret is not far-fetched. We have been able to structure a veritable niche for ourselves that pleases our Customers.

By Virtue of our quality food, excellent service delivery, assorted drinks, customer care and strategic management, we have been able to attract the patronage of notable personalities and dignitaries, both within and outside the country, as guests to our restaurant.

Twist's Spicy Foods is located at 27 Shirley Street, Highland Park, Queensland, Australia.
